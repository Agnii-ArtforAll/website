Mission


Agnii began as an entertainment group performing fire-dance and drum shows and today has metamorphosed into an ART for a CAUSE group, aspiring to create a culture of peace, friendship and harmony in the society. Agnii is driven by passion to see the world smile and dance with joy.

Agnii is a group which provides an amalgamation of various different artistes and talents showcasing their creativity using several props. And their props include fire, drums, and illusions. Saravana Dhanpal is the founder of the group Agnii. This talented lad was first introduced to the world of theatre and live performances. This inspired Saravana to increase the horizon and bring in more creative art work for the ACT™ community. He started the group Agnii in the year 2000 and since then he has entertained the audience along with his immensely talented group. The group offers activities like Drum Circles, Fire Dance, Drum Show, Theatre Performances and Mura Painting. Apart from these activities, Agnii is well-known for their hollow man act, stilt puppetry and Big Balloon Act.

Savarana Dhanpal had showcased his talent on television shows like India’s Got Talent and Entertainment Ke Liye Kuch Bhi Karega where he was well appreciated for his unique idea and talent. Agnii has performed at several international festivals across the globe. They participated in Swedish Youth cultural exchange programme in Sweden. Agnii represented India at the Global March against Child Labour in Italy and UK Indian Festival. Agnii has even volunteered for a six months program for United Nations. Agnii is very active with various NGO’s where they support and promote various social causes. They donated their income for building ‘Born Free School for underprivileged children’ and coordinated a rainwater harvesting project in a Government school.

Agnii had also conducted a special musical workshop for tribal children in collaboration with UNICEF. Agnii’s specialises in drum shows and performances. In drum circles, Agnii team engages the audience weather old or young and makes them jam with the drum beats. This activity is renowned in corporate vents as they engage the employees and the attendees with some practical activity. While the fire dance sets the stage ablaze with dancers playing with fire and musical tunes. Agnii bring out a unique list of activities which lightens the live act scenario for any event. Agnii can easily be compared with the international standards of live act performances.
